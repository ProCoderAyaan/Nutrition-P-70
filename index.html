Healthy and Unhealthy food

  • Healthy food Importance

    A healthy diet is needed for good health and nutrition. It protects you against many dangerous diseases. Eating a variety of foods and consuming less salt and sugar are needed for healthy diet.

  • Effects of Unhealthy food

    Any food that has no nutritional value can be considered as junk foods. Most of the fast foods, including candy, bakery products, burgers and sausages, salty and oily snacks or soft drinks can be classified as junk food. They have a high calorie content, salts and fats. The more we consume these, the less is the intake of essential nutrients and vitamins in the body.
